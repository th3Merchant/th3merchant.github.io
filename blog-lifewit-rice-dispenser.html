This dispenser is useful, but it is not tiny. It is meant to replace a bulk rice bag, so it needs a real home on a counter, pantry shelf, utility shelf, or cabinet area with enough height.

Before uploading it to your setup, check the dimensions against the exact spot where it will sit. A good storage product only helps if it fits the way your kitchen actually works.

The right buyer is not someone who cooks rice once a month. It is the household that reaches for rice often enough to want the process cleaner and easier.
