Do self-watering pots mean I never need to check the plant?

No. They can make watering easier, but plant type, soil, temperature, light, and root depth still matter.

Are these good for succulents?

Succulents often prefer drier soil. If using them in a self-watering pot, check moisture carefully and avoid keeping the reservoir constantly full.
