About Merchant

Merchant helps shoppers browse curated Amazon picks in a cleaner, easier way.

What Merchant Does

Merchant is a simple shopping guide that organizes selected Amazon products by category. Instead of browsing through endless product pages, visitors can quickly explore useful picks across sections such as Baby, Beauty & Personal Care, Pet Supplies, Home & Kitchen, Sports & Outdoors, and Best Sellers.

How Products Are Selected

Product selections are based on practical usefulness, category fit, customer-review patterns, everyday shopping appeal, and how easy the product is to understand at a glance.

  • Clear product purpose
  • Useful category fit
  • Strong or relevant customer feedback
  • Simple presentation for faster browsing
