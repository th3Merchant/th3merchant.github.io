Perfect Pairings

Pick the Right
Launcher for Your Ball.

Not all balls fit all launchers. Use this guide to make sure your Chuckit! ball and launcher are always perfectly matched.

🎯
Small Ball → Sport 14S
The 14S launcher is purpose-built for small (2") Chuckit! balls. Best for small breeds up to 20 lbs — perfect for indoor and small yard play.
🎾
Medium Ball → Sport 12M, 18M, 25M
The most versatile pairing. Medium (2.5") balls work with the Sport, Pro, Fetch & Fold, Junior, and Classic launchers. Compatible with glow-in-the-dark Max Glow versions too.
🏀
Large Ball → Pro 26L / Sport 26L
Large (3") balls for powerful 60–100 lb dogs. Pair with the 26L launchers for deep throws that really make your Labrador or German Shepherd sprint.
⚠️
XXL Balls — Standalone Only
XXL (4") balls are not compatible with any Chuckit! launcher — they're designed for manual throw only. Best for St Bernards and Cane Corsos who need the biggest ball possible.
